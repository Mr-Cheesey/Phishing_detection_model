USER GUIDANCE

Quick Safety Tips

✓

Check HTTPS carefully

HTTPS encrypts traffic but scammers use it too. Look for the real domain name.

!

Watch for brand spoofing

paypal-secure.xyz is NOT PayPal. The real domain is always paypal.com.

#

Avoid URL shorteners

bit.ly, tinyurl.com and similar services hide the real destination URL.

⚠

High entropy = suspicious

Random-looking domain names (e.g. xk3mf92.xyz) are often auto-generated by malware.
